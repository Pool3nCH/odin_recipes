PIZZA TIME BABY!

Ingredients

  • 300g pizza dough
  • 150g pepperoni, sliced
  • 200ml tomato sauce
  • 200g mozzarella, shredded
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 250°C (480°F) and place a baking sheet or pizza stone inside to heat up.
  2. On a floured surface, stretch or roll the dough into a round shape about 30cm in diameter.
  3. Brush the dough with olive oil, then spread the tomato sauce evenly, leaving a 2cm border for the crust.
  4. Scatter the mozzarella over the sauce, then layer the pepperoni on top. Season with oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Carefully transfer the pizza onto the hot baking sheet or stone and bake for 10–12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  6. Remove from the oven, let rest for 2 minutes, slice, and serve.
