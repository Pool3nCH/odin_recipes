Jambalaya

Recipe description

Jambalaya, an easy recipe for the whole family. Make it with shrimps, sausage or other fish, meat or seafood!

Ingredients

  • 300g chicken thighs, diced
  • 200g andouille sausage, sliced
  • 200g shrimp, peeled
  • 1.5 cups long grain white rice
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 400g canned diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp Cajun seasoning
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Brown the chicken and sausage for 5–6 minutes until golden. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, sauté onion, celery, bell pepper, and garlic for 4–5 minutes until softened.
  3. Return the meat to the pot. Add rice, broth, tomatoes, and Cajun seasoning. Stir well and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20–25 minutes until the rice absorbs the liquid.
  5. Stir in the shrimp, cover, and cook for 5 more minutes until pink and cooked through.
  6. Remove from heat, rest for 5 minutes, and serve.
